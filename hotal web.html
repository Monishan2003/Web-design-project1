Coral Moon Hotel

A Haven of Luxury in Sri Lanka

Coral Moon Hotel offers a blend of modern luxury and warm Sri Lankan hospitality. Guests can indulge in exquisite cuisine at our signature restaurant, unwind with handcrafted cocktails at the rooftop bar, and relax in the serene ambiance of our exclusive lounge. Whether you're here for business or leisure, Coral Moon Hotel promises an exceptional stay infused with comfort and elegance.
